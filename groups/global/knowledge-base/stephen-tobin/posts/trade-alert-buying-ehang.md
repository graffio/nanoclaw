# Trade Alert: Buying Ehang

*Considering ABAT*

> Returning to one of my favorite stocks, the profits from trading this in 2022 paid for my RV, I closed all trades last year at over $20 and wrote an article explaining the pause. I think it is time to buy again. I may well write a new article in the coming days about this opportunity.
> 
> Ehang has delivered an outstanding quarter showing real improvement. The price of the EH216 has been set at $400,000 which is very low and should drive sales much higher in the coming years. They are expecting the full manufacturing approval in the coming quarter and sold over 20 units in Q4 and delivered positive cash flow. EH opened an office in spain and have signed a deal with Telefonica to expand the autonomous software into Europe and South America.

> Key highlights as follows:

1.  **Total Revenues**:
    
    -   Q4 2023: RMB56.6 million (US$8.0 million), a **260.9% YoY increase**.
        
    -   Fiscal Year 2023: Significant growth with revenues up by **165.0%**.
        
2.  **Gross Margin**:
    
    -   Q4 2023: **64.7%**.
        
3.  **Operating Loss**:
    
    -   Q4 2023: **RMB75.2 million** (US$10.6 million), an **18.4% improvement** from Q4 2022.
        
    -   Adjusted operating loss (non-GAAP): **RMB24.9 million**, a **59.4% improvement**.
        
4.  **Net Loss**:
    
    -   Q4 2023: **RMB72.5 million** (US$10.2 million), a **34.2% improvement** from Q4 2022.
        
    -   Adjusted net loss (non-GAAP): **RMB22.1 million**, a **62.8% improvement**.
        
5.  **Operational Highlights**:
    
    -   Sales and deliveries of EH216 series products: **23 units** in Q4 2023.
        
    -   Positive cash flow from operations during Q4 2023.
        
    -   Obtained the world’s first **Pilotless Passenger-Carrying eVTOL Type Certificate** for EH216-S from the CAAC.
        
    -   Expanded to the UAE market.
        

Technicals suggest a target of $50, we can expect some pullback as the price looks overbought but the recent low of $10, marked by the orange wave ((IV))? should hold.
