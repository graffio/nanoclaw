# Note -- May 15, 2025

Electrovaya is Powering Up

I have been investing in Electrovaya since May 2022. Back then, it was a tiny loss-making company with negative equity, but the quality of its technology and the value of its sustainable competitive advantage was clear. I bought it last November, December, and March, and my faith is being rewarded.

Q2 2025 results are in :

Revenue Soars: $15.0 million: a phenomenal 40% increase over 2024.

Orders Fly: $25 million received, a record.

Robust Gross Margins: 31.1% in Q2 2025.

Eight Quarters of + EBITDA: $2.0 million in Q2.

Net Profitability: Turned a corner with a net profit of $0.8 million, up from a net loss of $0.8 million in the prior year.

Strong Balance Sheet: debt dropped by $5 million to $13.1 million.

Strategic Financing: closed a $51 million Loan from EXIM.

Expansion: US battery assembly started.

These results are more than just numbers; they represent a commitment to innovation, efficiency, and delivering exceptional value. I am incredibly optimistic about the future and look forward to continued overperformance and high returns from what is now my second-largest investment.
