*Adding Byrna Technologies to the Strong Buy Portfolio*

I will add BYRN when today's market opens. Byrna, along with CDRE (which I already own), is part of my non-lethal arms sector.

I spoke with the CEO of BYRN yesterday as part of my due diligence process. In the next few days, I will publish an article on SeekingAlpha containing a detailed view of my research.

If you are not familiar with BYRN, they manufacture a nonlethal CO2 handgun that is legal to own and carry in the US and sold directly to consumers. The gun fires pellets that contain irritant chemicals and will provide stopping power at distances up to 60ft. They have sold thousands of these guns and so far have not had a fatal injury inflicted.

I think BYRN is a potential multi-bagger and will add it to my Strong-Buy portfolio.
