# Trade Alert: Buying CDRE before earnings

*Excellent time to buy this growing dividend payer*

Cadre Holdings (CDRE) is one of the non-lethal defense companies I mentioned in the post about Joby in February. I have been looking for an entry point since then, and today is likely to be the best day to buy into this sector with huge growth potential.

Cadre makes holsters, body armor, and related products for first responders, the military, and private individuals. I think they are about to enter a boom time in today's climate. Cadre's management has proven its ability to build conglomerates. They built Armor from almost zero and sold it to BAE for $4.5 billion. They bought back a piece of Armor called Safari Holdings for $125 million and have started building again. The company pays dividends, has a solid balance sheet, and consistently outperforms guidance.

Q1 results are due today, and I have bought in advance.

The technical chart is very promising. A low may have just been recorded, and a return to the ongoing upward trend looks likely. I expect this to be a longer-term holding and have submitted an article to SeekingAlpha explaining my reasoning in detail.
