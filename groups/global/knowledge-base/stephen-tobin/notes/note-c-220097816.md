# Note -- February 26, 2026

$ATAI released phase 2 data for its social anxiety drug, they claimed a win but the stock is down. ATAI quoted a one tail P-Value of 0.036 which translates to the more normal two tailed value of 0.07, the FDA requires 0.05 so this is a big miss. The 0.05 means that the result has less than 5% chance of being due to other causes than the drug.
