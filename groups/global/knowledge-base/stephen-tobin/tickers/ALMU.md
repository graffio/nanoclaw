## Company Overview

Aeluma is a photonics semiconductor company developing quantum dot lasers and InGaAs-on-silicon technology. Technology uses 200mm/300mm silicon substrates, offering cost advantages over legacy 3" InP wafers. Also developing high-speed photodetectors targeting 100G to 400G per lane using wafer-scale manufacturing.
