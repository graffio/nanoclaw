# Trade Alert: Buying SES.AI

*300 Share mid market order issue*

Following my post yesterday I bought QBTS and have today issued a midprice order to buy 300 shares in SES.AI (SES).

The fundamental change is unchaged but in review.

SES.AI has transitioned from a pre-revenue company to an emerging one, thanks to commercial validation of its technology. The company's core strength lies in its AI-driven electrolyte discovery platform, which recently yielded a new solvent that significantly improves battery performance. This breakthrough has led to multiple orders, including a $10 million order from auto OEM partners.

SES has also launched an enhanced 2170 battery with increased power and a longer cycle life, and is progressing with its lithium metal anode pouch cell development for the eVTOL market. The company's AI is also used for manufacturing and safety, with plans to monetize this technology in the future. While risks remain due to its early commercial stage, SES has a strong financial position with ample cash runway.
