## Dropping Sectors

My trading plan is all about emerging technology, and the range of sectors I cover changes.

I have removed the CRM sector from my coverage universe after conducting in-depth research into HubSpot and Salesforce. The industry has become too fragmented, price competitive, and barriers to entry have collapsed.

I am also dropping “software for energy management” following a deep dive into Applied Energy Group. AI appears to have removed barriers to entry in many of these software areas, increasing competition and removing margins. Vertical integration is becoming more common, and I feel these software providers are going to come under increasing market pressure with the ongoing erosion of their IP.

If a technology no longer has the potential to disrupt and deliver large profits, then it also loses the potential for outsized share price appreciation.
