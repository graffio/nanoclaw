# Trade Alert: Buying PONY, Closing TBLA

*First RoboTaxi Investment, more to come!*

Yesterday's 30% rise was clear evidence that the downtrend is over, and now is the time to buy into the fastest-growing highest potential emerging technology in the world today.

I will place a mid-price order for 60 shares in Pony.AI (PONY) before the market opens today. That will be approximately 3% of the demonstration account's equity value.

I will also close Taboola (TBLA) with a mid-market sell order.

# PONY The Buy Thesis

These are the RoboTaxi forms I have been investigating over the last month. I covered the size of this market and the growth projected in my recent [weekly review post](https://open.substack.com/pub/stephentobin/p/weekly-review?r=nh85d&utm_campaign=post&utm_medium=web&showWelcomeOnShare=false).

Pony is

-   RoboTaxi First,
    
-   China First,
    
-   Tier 1 Cities First.
    

I like the focus; it offers the quickest route to scaled operations and keeps Pony away from competition with Waymo.

Pony aims to scale its taxi fleet from 250 vehicles to over 2,000 by 2026, capitalizing on the immense population and favorable regulatory environment in China's Tier 1 cities.

Bank of America says it will hit 68,000 taxis by 2029, and the Wall Street average price target is $21.

Plans include integrating with ride-hailing apps, including their own, and with popular [Chinese mapping applications like AMAP](https://www.scmp.com/tech/tech-trends/article/3282430/ponyai-teams-alibabas-amap-unit-expand-robotaxi-service-china). Plus, selling vehicles to other operators in partnership with three [OEMs, Toyota, GAC, and BAIC](https://markets.ft.com/data/announce/detail?dockey=1330-9437321en-4975OSVAKFUV16HA2PMH6L1JRL#:~:text=PONY%20AI%20Inc.,%E2%80%93%20Company%20Announcement%20%2D%20FT.com).

For Trucks: It’s a longer-term, slow build strategy. The robotruck division is gaining momentum, with revenue exceeding their taxi services, but it is still in the testing phase. Pony recently received [approval for 1+N platooning](https://ir.pony.ai/news-releases/news-release-details/pony-ai-inc-becomes-first-company-china-approved-autonomous) on major highways.

### Negatives

Pony reported a 62% drop in robotaxi service [revenue for fiscal year 2025](https://ir.pony.ai/news-releases/news-release-details/pony-ai-inc-announces-unaudited-fourth-quarter-and-full-year), leading to a decline in its stock price and multiple law firms announcing investigations into potential class action claims. Significant short-selling activity followed the earnings release, intensifying the drop in value.

The legal claims and the short selling are without merit. We are getting used to this anti-Chinese sentiment, and often it is not based on any fundamental business reasons.

While total revenue declined in 2024 due to the expiration of some R&D licensing agreements and the end of JD payment milestones, income from taxi fares and robotruck services increased significantly.

The drop in revenue was a sign that the company is moving out of the R&D phase and into its commercialization stage.

### Finances

Despite having a strong cash position, Pony is expected to raise further capital, and we will face dilution as it scales its operations. The chart below shows yesterday’s sudden uptick, and I do expect some pullback in the coming days. I will likely exit if the downtrend resumes with a close below $3.92; my initial target will be a new high above $24.

It is not yet practical to determine a fair value. I don't have information about margins, the size of operations, costs, or revenue to build a mathematical model, but I will do so later this year.
