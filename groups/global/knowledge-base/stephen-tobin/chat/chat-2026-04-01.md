## Reply: HSAI entry point — golden cross / EMA crossovers
*Thread 0e9956fd-8c5d-443a-9881-5178f3c8dfa5*

**Context (Roland ROJAS):**
> Hsai is giving a good entry point right now ema 9 is breaking ema 200 upside

**Stephen Tobin:**
I don't like to trade these crosses, I think they are generally false signals. The famed death crosses and golden crosses lead to losses more than 75% of the time. In deed if you bought every death cross in the S&P since inception you would have shown a big profit. These crosses generally imply a short term top or bottom is close so a golden cross appears just before a top and a death cross just before a bottom. The opposite of what people suggest
