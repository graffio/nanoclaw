# Portfolio Adjustment: Closing META

*Taking profits on Facebook*

It has been a very active day so far, I have opened QBTS and CLosed META, and I am currently working on one more trade.

Facebook has been in the take-profit area for some time. Now seems like a good time to book this first profit of 2025.

META did not make the progress I expected with the digital human product, which was the primary catalyst for my investment. I worry that the recent announcements will end fact-checking on the platform. This is reminiscent of the changes made to Twitter, which caused an exodus of advertisers.

The agreement with eBay kept me in the trade longer than expected, but I am not convinced it will generate enough profit to keep the share price at these high levels.

The chart below shows the entry and exit points. The exit would appear to agree with the EW count, but the forecast does not imply the share price will fall that much. However, I think there are better places to put money at the moment.
