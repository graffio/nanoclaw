# Note -- July 4, 2025

I can't explain the price movements in quantum stocks; they seem detached from reality. 

However, I do think QBTS has technology far beyond the capability of the rest of the market. I honestly do not believe RGTI, IONQ, and the rest actually have working technology. When I say working, I mean technology that customers can use it to solve a problem. 

As you know, QBTS annealing technology is an entirely different architecture that relies on a different quantum process from the rest.
 QBTS is creating an ASIC, or application-specific integrated circuit, that performs a single function. 
The others are attempting to develop a universal chip that will perform all functions. 

It's like comparing a laptop to a crypto mining rig. You would not buy a mining rig to watch Netflix, but if you were a miner, you would not be interested in anything else. 

QBTS has an ASIC chip that works; it solves large optimization problems and nothing else.
The rest have a universal chip that doesn't work, it can't currently do anything because of the errors it produces.
