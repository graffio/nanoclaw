# Trade Alert: Buying WNNR

*BITF closed for 50%, February another winning month*

WNNR is a SPAC expected to take Zapata Inc. to the NYSE.

ZAPATA is a generative AI development company; on February 8th, Zapata and D Wave Quantum Computers announced a strategic alliance to bring generative AI software based on D waves annealing quantum computers to the market. The announcement started a jump in D Wave, that I unfortunately missed having closed my D Wave position in January.

Zapata has published several scientific papers on Quantum, especially in the two important areas of error correction and measurement against classical computers.

Zapata is particularly strong in developing compressed Large Language Models; this is the key technology that will drive Generative AI. They have a platform that can develop company-specific chatbots trained on internal documents and run them on the world's fastest computers, including D waves Quantum devices.

D Wave is making excellent technical progress with its annealing computers, significantly expanding the size of its computing power and improving error correction. The newly released Advantage 2 machine has 1,200 qubits, making it 20 times faster than the previous machine. D wave now sells its machines as quantum hybrid solvers and appears to be perfecting the hybrid classical/quantum machine for their annealing machine.

D wave is the only company with a commercially available quantum computer; it is limited in scope as it can only solve optimization problems; however, that is the key tool for generative AI. Zapata may be able to turn this hardware into the most important software of the next few years. I think their impact could be of the Palantir scale!

This is a risky investment; the merger is not yet complete, and we have not had the full details of the plans. However, I will take a position today.
