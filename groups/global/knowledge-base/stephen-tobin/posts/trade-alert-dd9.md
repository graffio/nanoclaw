# Trade alert;

After a full review I have decided to book a loss on EVTL Vertical Aerospace. Yesterday’s earnings make difficult reading and likely push the timeline out beyond 2028. I was hoping for “successful completion of transition” but just got continuing to work through transition testing. It might be an issue. The change of aircraft to the Valo brought in new orders which is good and the new design seems better able to fulfill the machine brief. Advancing the battery line is also positive but they failed to raise enough cash and have decided not to sign a manufacturing partner like Joby and Archer making the cost of production higher and the learning curve steeper. Quite likely this is going lower.

One important point- I could be selling at a low so please make an independent decision.

the stock has fallen below my 40% cut off and I would need a very good reason to keep it, I could not find any such reason in the SEC filling.
