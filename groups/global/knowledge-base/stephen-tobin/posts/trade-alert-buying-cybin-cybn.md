# Trade ALert: Buying Cybin (CYBN)

*Low value multibagger potential*

Cybin is one of the companies in the Hundred those companies I follow with the potential to increase by more than 100%

# Key Investment Points

It is worth noting that the entire space is increasing; ATAI, in particular, has been on a tear, doubling its value in recent weeks. Cybin is much smaller but has a similar drug pipeline and may be closer to market than ATAI; it is not on the radar of most Analysts and currently receives little press. There is a good chance this is about to change.

Cybin has the most experienced and successful CEO of all the psychedelic companies. Doug Drysdale is a respected industry veteran who has built drug companies before. As long as he is with Cybin, I am interested in buying.

Cybin has made excellent progress in the last year; it has continued to strengthen its portfolio of drugs by acquiring competitors that are low on cash. It bought Small Pharma in October in an all-share transaction. Small Pharma is a UK-based drug developer with an advanced program looking at short-duration psychedelics. The duration time is vital as it reduces clinic time and, hence, the cost of treatment, making any approved drug more affordable for the insurance and medical companies. Small Pharma is working on DMT, and the companies have a broad portfolio of products. The first Small pharma drug is due to market in 2027 the same time as Cybin's key offering CYB-003, which has a good chance of being a billion-dollar blockbuster.

In October, the patent office confirmed the patents on Cybin's main drug offerings (including the two mentioned above), this is a big deal and significantly increases the value of the pipeline.

## Catalyst for trading

Q1 2024 looks like a defining moment.

In October, Cybin announced positive Phase 2 results from its deuterated psilocybin (CYB-003); the drug met all its endpoints and will progress to a Phase 3 trial this year. The meeting with the FDA to agree this is due Q1 2024 and the first patient should be dosed this quarter. It will be a big moment.

Phase 1 data for 3 drug programs will be released this quarter. Each will have an impact and add to the momentum.

Each of these milestones, if positive, will boost values

## Valuation

The analytics firm Global Data reviewed several drug companies and picked two Cybin drugs as having great potential. They Said

CYB-003, a deuterated psilocybin analog, in phase 2 for major depressive disorder. Psilocybin is a naturally occurring psychedelic compound found in "magic mushrooms." The drug could have a US launch December 2027 with 2029 sales of $1.4B.

SPL-026, in phase 2 for major depression, contains dimethyltryptamine, which is found in plants and some animals. The expected US launch is in 2027, followed by 2028 in the European Union. Revenue of $0.9B is projected in 2029.

The work from Global Data follows the changes to guidance at the FDA and the recently announced testing results.

Cybin has 4 Wall Street analysts covering it who give a 1-year price target of $5.18, and they are closely aligned with less than 15% difference between the highest and lowest value. It represents a more than 1,000% increase in 12 months.
