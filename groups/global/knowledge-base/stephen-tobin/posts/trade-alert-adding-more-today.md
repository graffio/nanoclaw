## Trade Alert #85: Adding to Pony.AI

Excellent earnings from PONY (Nasdaq: PONY) (SEHK: 2026) this morning, they are on track to move to 1,000 robotaxis this year and 3,000 next year.

Revenue grew 72% year over year, and more importantly, the robotaxi operations in Guangzhou moved to city-wide breakeven. This vindicates the entire business model and the expansion plans should see this company become a huge profit maker in the medium term.

The newly released plan to adopt an asset-light strategy in partnership with Sunlight Mobility should enable a speedy rollout of the new Gen-7 robotaxi. Sunlight is a significant mobility company in China with operations in almost 200 cities. They have a large user base and an established online platform. The fact that they are prepared to fund the robotaxis in partnership with PONY is important and paves the way for future similar deals. The first Taxis under the agreement will be operational before the end of this year.

PONY continues to reduce the Bill of Materials for each robotaxi, after a 70% drop last year, they guided to a further 20% this year.

The operational footprint continues to grow. They now have 7 cities in China and have added Qatar to the international list along with Singapore, Luxembourg, and South Korea

In a second development, PONY announced on November 19th that two OEMs in China will start manufacturing its Gen-4 Autonomous trucks. SANY and Dongfeng will produce the Gen 4 at the thousand-unit scale for fleet deployment in 2026. SANT is an existing partner, while Dongfeng is new, demonstrating the continued growth across all aspects of this business.

We have been treating PONY and WRD as a single investment as they are closely correlated and are direct competitors. I will add a half-size position of $250 today (approximately 1.5% of equity), bringing the total investment in PONY to approx $650, my maximum is 5% ($850)

## Trade Alert #86: Adding to Hesai

Hesai (Nasdaq: HSAI) (SEHK: 2525) held a technology open day yesterday, prompting multiple analysts to raise price targets and sending the share price breaking out of a brutal pullback. The stock rose 18% but is still 40% below its late September peak. The company unveiled a new system on a chip LiDAR, a proprietary “Photon Isolation” technology which improves safety by removing cross-contamination effects, and a new version of its best-selling ATX LiDAR. The ATX is designed for assisted driving not autonomous operations. Since launch in Q1 more than a million units have shipped, and a backlog of 4 million units was announced for this long range high resolution unit

Cumulative deliveries have now passed 185 million units for hesai, putting them in a class of their own for automotive LiDAR.

The pattern we are trading with previous trades is shown below, as I said in the weekend review, LiDAR is no longer an emerging technology. All technologies go through this, but thanks mainly to Hesai, LiDAR has become a staple of new cars in many parts of the world. Both premium and lower price models ship with the technology as standard, and it has become as crucial to safety as an airbag.

All previous Hesai positions are shown below. We have had some great results, but the two most recent trades are underwater. I will add today, expecting the stock to reach an all-time high early in 2026, and then move on to my original target of $50. It will probably be my final LiDAR trade.

I will add $250 worth of stocks, the same half size as with PONY today and WRD yesterday.
