# Trade Alert: Buying SES.AI

*Upgrading on commercial traction*

I have just finished an interview with Qichao Hu, CEO and founder of SES.AI (SES). SES has been on our watchlist for more than 2 years, and I have written about them twice on seeking alpha, rating them a buy both times. I was unable to give a strong buy rating due to the lack of commercial traction, but it was always clear they had some excellent technology.

It is now clear that significant commercial traction is taking place with multiple orders from new and existing clients.

The key development is their newly discovered solvent, which, when turned into an electrolyte, makes a substantial difference to both SES Li-Metal anode batteries, traditional Li-ion anodes, and the new all-silicon anodes. They have announced a $10 million order from their existing EV customers for this new solvent and multiple orders from drone makers and robot companies.

The technical Chart is very promising and suggests now is a great time to buy. A wave 2 low is in, and oversold conditions exis.t
