# Following the Plan and reducing exposure

*Risk Off persists*

A general risk-off sentiment is pervading the market, with most indices pointing lower; it's probably time to take some money off the table.

Following the plans posted earlier this week, **I will close AIRO Group Holdings,** resulting in a loss of 40%. There is always the possibility that I am closing at the low, and the stock will recover; however, I have decided to take the hit now and deposit the cash raised from the sale into the account.

**Aurora (AUR) has fallen by 30% and I will also close here.** Again, I could be closing at a low, but Aurora is a long way from profit generation, and there will be further opportunities to buy this stock.

To protect capital, I will also **close Ceres Power Holdings (CWR) or CRPHY.** I never like such vertical price action and will look to buy back in on a pullback.

If the current trading conditions persist, it will likely be the first of several changes as I seek to preserve capital for the inevitable opportunities that will emerge.

The new holdings of BYRN and DroneShield have fallen sharply in the first few days they have been open. I may decide to cut these two, but I would prefer to hold on to them in the hope that this is more of a short-term move.
