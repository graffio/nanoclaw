**Investing in Hesai: Leading the LiDAR Revolution for RoboTaxis**

Hesai Technology has emerged as the clear leader in the LiDAR systems market, particularly for RoboTaxis. This dominance is underscored by its significant market share and strategic partnerships.

Several compelling factors drive me to buy Hesai.

Firstly, Hesai has successfully transitioned to large-scale production, which is crucial for achieving economies of scale. This shift not only enhances their production efficiency but also positions them to drive profitability within the next two years.

Moreover, Hesai has secured partnerships with several high-profile original equipment manufacturers (OEMs) in both China and Europe. These collaborations include exclusive agreements, further solidifying their market position.

Hesai’s leadership is also reflected in their impressive market share, they may be the dominant supplier. This success is attributed to their low cost devices and strategic alliances with major players in the autonomous driving sector.

Hesai is a strategic move for me, playing the second dimension, as my father used to say. Electric vehicles are taking off more in China than anywhere else, and Hesai is a Chinese based LiDAR company that will benefit from the take-up. It allows me exposure to Chinese EV sales without buying an overpriced Chinese EV maker.

I will be writing a detailed report on SeekingAlpha and hope to be able to add the company to my StrongBuy portfolio.
