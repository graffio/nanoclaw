    Compass Pathways (CMPS) priced its public offering of 17.5M ADSs at $8.00 per ADS. It also offered pre-funded warrants to purchase up to 1.25M ADSs at $7.9999 each to certain institutional investors, with underwriters granted a 30-day option for an additional 2.8125M ADSs.

    The net proceeds will fund ongoing Phase 3 trials COMP005 and COMP006 (likely for treatment-resistant depression), a Phase 2b/3 trial of COMP360 psilocybin for PTSD, commercial readiness acceleration, and general corporate purposes.
