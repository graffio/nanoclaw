## PONY

The trade taken in April is up 185%, showing a 3% increase over the week, but is off its all-time high after a 15% fall on Friday. A period of consolidation appears to have started with sideways movement for the last 10 days. Technical traders will recognise an inside candle pattern on Thursday and Friday, typical of price consolidation. The chart shows the entry and subsequent price action.

My strategy is all about comparing the different companies in each emerging technology sector. When I compare the price action of PONY against its most immediate peer company, WeRide (WRD), the possibility of mean retracement seems likely. Mean retracement is when two previously highly correlated prices, those moving together, break that relationship for a small time period but then resume it.

[

![Image 3](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/d6d33f91-2cac-4636-8ac4-df54bcc743a5_1122x744.png)



](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!sUCN!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fd6d33f91-2cac-4636-8ac4-df54bcc743a5_1122x744.png)

The two stocks have been highly correlated in the past, with WRD typically demanding a price premium.

While Pony.ai and WeRide were initially rated as "Buy" prospects, Pony.ai's focused commercial strategy—prioritizing robotaxis, the Chinese market, and Tier 1 cities—offers a more straightforward path to profitability.

WeRide's broader approach, encompassing taxis, buses, and road sweepers across global markets without a dedicated app, presents a less defined trajectory to profitability and carries higher operational costs. Despite this, WeRide remains a potentially good investment prospect, particularly given its recent positive developments.

However, further review is needed to ascertain its profitability timeline compared to Pony.ai's near-term break-even outlook. The differing strategies highlight why Pony.ai was the initial investment after both received "Buy" ratings.

I will update via a trade alert after I have completed another in-depth review of WeRide, considering the effect of the following news items on its profitability prospects.

-   Expansion with UBER to 15 cities
    
-   Robotaxi operations started in Abu Dhabi
    
-   Guangzhou 24/7 license and operations
    
-   Guangzhou Robobus service
    
-   Beijing commercial area expansion
    
-   France deployment, the first robotaxi license in the country
    
-   Robobus deployment at Zurich Airport
    

A mean retracement could be Pony moving lower, WeRide moving higher, or both, or the two moving higher together. If I decide to take any action, I will update you via a trade alert.
