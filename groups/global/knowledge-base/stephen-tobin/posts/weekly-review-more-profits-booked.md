## **Placing Orders**

A couple of people have mentioned they are having trouble getting the same entry point as I do. I thought I would make it clear how I place orders, anyone can follow the same technique and will get the same price.

I have taken some screenshots of the process showing how I will place the order to buy SES tomorrow. (Note this is an example I will send a trade alert tomorrow when I confirm everything is OK to press ahead).

I will place the order pre market. When I open SES to place an order it shows these settings
