# Note -- May 13, 2025

The NHTSA has written to Tesla requesting information about its Robotaxi operation. specifically asking about “safety of its robotaxi operations in reduced visibility conditionssuch as: sun glare, fog, dust, rain or snow.” We all know it is not safe becasue it only has cameras. The letter also asked for information about the difference between FSD and the robotaxi software, which cars will be used in the robotaxi fleet and how safety will be recorded. As a result PONY is down 17%, it has nothing to do with the letter or Tesla but all ships move with the tide. I hope Tesla publishes its response but I very much doubt it.
