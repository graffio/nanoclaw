## The Trading Strategy

Everyone needs a strategy; without one, you could end up chasing every move in the market.

In the UK, brokers are legally required to disclose the percentage of retail investors who make or lose money. One of my UK brokers, IG Markets, publishes monthly figures on the percentage of retail investors who lose money, and in December, for the first time in 2025, it fell below 75%. This screenshot is from their homepage.

[

![Image 4](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/bea64ce3-8470-4fd0-a335-a45f37b6575e_624x46.png)

Most investors, independent analysts and influencers lose money. That is one reason they do not publish their trading history transparently. To get on the SeekingAlpha top analyst list, you only need to be right 60% of the time and have an average return of 50%, which makes you one of the best 15 analysts out of the 10,000 on the platform. It shows just how difficult making money is and to do it consistently you need to consistently apply a profitable plan.

My strategy limits the type of company I can buy to small-cap emerging technology stocks. This focus is based on my understanding and expertise in this specific area; deviating from it risks diminishing my success. I must resist the urge to invest in other groups of companies, no matter how appealing, as I lack confidence in achieving the same returns outside my core competency.

The project to turn $250 into $100K puts a strong limit on the number of stocks I can hold and the amount of money available to trade with. This restriction forces me to make decisions, I have to exit some stocks to buy others, I must book profits and cut losses.

The strategy has proven its worth, we are over half way through the project and still above target. I will continue to follow all the rules I have developed and subscribers can expect much the same as we have seen over the last two years. Which is

Around 20 holdings of small caps stocks

About 3 new names and two add on trades a month

Approximately 4 exits per month

High volatility with individual monthly returns ranging from -14% to +30%

Please remember past results don’t guarantee future returns.

There are many profitable ways to trade and many subscribers use my work to inform their trading rather than following every trading action. The subscriber base is broad, ranging from new traders to incorporated family offices.

Several Subscribers hold the stocks I recommend for much longer time frames than I do and are doing extremely well. They operate under different constraints and aim to maximize returns based on their risk tolerance and available capital.
