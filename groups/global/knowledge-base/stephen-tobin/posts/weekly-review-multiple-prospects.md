## Outlook

There has been a lot of movement in several of the technologies I follow.

**LiDAR** companies have started to announce series production wins

**eVTOL** is approaching commercial operations, EVE and EVTL making interesting progress

**Electric vans** are reporting growing orders, REE, WKHS, XOS are of interest.

**RF manufacturers** are moving higher as a group. STM, QCOM,KEYS, AMPG are my targets

Mixed **Recycling companies** are leading this sector higher but ABAT and PCT have made notable business progress.

It all means the prospect list is extremely full at the moment, probably the largest number of prospects in the last three years. I will review all the sectors listed this month.

I will be looking for commercial progress, not share price appreciation or business progress. QS made excellent business progress with the commissioning of its new machine, and SLDP made significant progress when BMW produced a test car that ran on SLDP cells. This is not what I am looking for, I want to see commercial progress, **new orders-increased revenue-break even operations,** it is commercial progress that will drive our profits.

I will continue to actively manage all positions and exit holdings when necessary, moving to companies with better medium-term potential if the opportunity arises.

I am not loyal to companies and will exit regardless of past wins, the quality of tech, or relationships with management. ROI first and ROI last.
