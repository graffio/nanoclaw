Flash Joule Heating (FJH) provides several technological and economic advantages over conventional metal recovery methods:
1. No chemical reagents – FJH requires no strong acids or bases, eliminating costly neutralization, wastewater treatment, and environmental compliance steps.
2. No smelting – The process avoids high-temperature furnaces; metals are released through ultra-fast electrical heating within milliseconds.
3. Low energy demand – Only the material itself is heated, not the entire mass, leading to significantly lower energy consumption.
4. Speed – Metal liberation occurs in seconds instead of hours or days.
5. High yield and purity – Metals are recovered in oxide or elemental form with high extraction efficiency.
6. Broad feedstock applicability – Effective on e-waste, industrial residues, and natural ores.
7. Modular scalability – Capacity can be increased by running multiple reactors in parallel without efficiency loss.
8. Minimal emissions – No fossil fuel combustion, no toxic by-products.
