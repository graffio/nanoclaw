# Note -- February 2, 2026

Deleverage the word of the day. It means people have to sell assets to cover the losses they have incurred from the collapse in Gold/Silver/BTC. Another interpretation is moving money from the poor to the rich. Retail traders sell stocks to Hedge funds at a discount to cover losses they made buying gold from hedge funds at inflated prices.
