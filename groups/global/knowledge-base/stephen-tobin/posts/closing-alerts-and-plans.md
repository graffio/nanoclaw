# Closing Alerts and Plans

*Sudden moves require action*

We have had a very profitable few weeks, and I am now preparing to book some profits and limit some losses. Several of the trades I am preparing to exit may become future trades.

It is always important to book profits and take losses. It is a key part of the plan to recycle capital and increase position size, allowing compounding to take effect.

The charts below show entry points in green and exit points in red; they are not exact, as I add them to my automatic Elliott wave software package.

Trades requiring attention are shown below.
