# Trade Alert: Buying Hesai

*Trying to get in below $12 for 100% upside*

We closed Hesai for more than 200% return earlier this year, and since then the stock has fallen significantly.

Hesai released a new set of LiDAR products today that extends its dominance in the field of low-cost automotive LiDAR, but also opens new markets. The new products are complete solutions for autonomous driving rather than just single LiDAR sensors. They will significantly extend the total market for Hesai and lead to substantial upgrades to price targets.

The full range of new products [can be viewed here](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/hesai-unveils-infinity-eye-lidar-solution-for-l2-l3-and-l4-autonomous-driving-systems-302433328.html)

This new product comes after news of a new [volume producer, Zeeker,](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/hesai-and-zeekr-mark-milestone-in-strategic-partnership-lidar-equipped-as-standard-on-zeekr-007gt-302430253.html) signing with Hesai for its latest car, and the confirmation that Hesai will be supplying DiDi’s first mass[\-produced RoboTaxi](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/hesais-at128-powers-didi-and-gac-aions-first-mass-produced-l4-robotaxi-302428922.html).

These new products and deals point to a revenue beat in 2025.

My original target of $28 remains valid; I have not yet been able to rework the mathematical model as I am waiting for audited accounts.

I have read through the recent short seller report in detail and believe it to be a complete work of fiction.

I will issue a mid-price buy order for 30 shares on the demonstration account. (That will be approximately 3.5% of the portfolio.) and will report the price I get when it fills.
