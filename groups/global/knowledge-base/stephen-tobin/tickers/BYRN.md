## Company Overview

Byrna Technologies manufactures non-lethal personal security devices, primarily a CO2-powered handheld launcher that fires chemical irritant pellets. Legal to own and carry in the US, with stopping power at distances up to 60 feet. Part of Stephen's "non-lethal arms" sector alongside Cadre (CDRE).

**Products:**

- Byrna SD/LE launchers (consumer and law enforcement)
- Byrna CL launcher (new product launched FY2025)
- Chemical irritant pellets and accessories

**Revenue model:** Direct-to-consumer sales plus growing retail distribution.

- FY2024: Strong year, BYRN was a top performer (+144%)
- FY2025: \$118.0M full-year revenue (+38% YoY)
- FY Q4 2025: \$35.1M (+26% YoY)
- Black Friday/Cyber Monday 2024: 1,000% increase in sales

**Key events:** CEO Bryan Ganz retired in early March 2026, replaced by Conn Davies. A short seller report hit BYRN in November 2024 but Stephen found "nothing of concern" after line-by-line review.

## Investment Thesis

**Original thesis (Aug 2024):** "Potential multi-bagger." Non-lethal CO2 handgun legal to own and carry in the US. Spoke with CEO as part of due diligence. Published SeekingAlpha article. Part of non-lethal arms sector alongside CDRE.

**Later developments:** Short seller report in Nov 2024 — Stephen investigated line by line, found nothing contradicting management. Relationship with company deteriorated when they expected him to ignore the short report. By Dec 2024, position was up 187% with manufacturing expanding to Latin America.

**Thesis deterioration (2025-2026):** Revenue growth slowed. Q4 FY2025 preliminary results showed revenue below consensus. CEO change added uncertainty. Stephen in Mar 2026: "I am not sure it is about the new CEO, more the lacklustre results last quarter."

**Exit thesis (Mar 2026):** Oil crisis / Middle East escalation. "Major concerns with Byrna — the soft earnings report this quarter may be a sign that consumer spending is already slowing and their high-priced devices depend solely on discretionary retail spending." Exited all positions on 2026-03-19.
