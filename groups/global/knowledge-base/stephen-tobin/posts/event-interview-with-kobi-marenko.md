# EVENT: Interview with Kobi Marenko

*CEO of Arbe discussing, prospects for 2025.*

I was lucky enough to interview Kobi again today. We are already long ARBE and wanted to learn about progress and the path towards commercialization. Kobi had a lot of important things to say about the timings of new deals and the beginning of production. We also discussed financing for ARBE, and Kobi was keen to stress that he believes they now have enough cash to fund the business until the start of production.

Kobi also gave his views on the industry generally and the use of LiDAR and made some interesting points about the recent Innoviz/MobilEye deal.

You can watch the full video below. I will write a new article in the new year and consider whether we need to take any action then.
