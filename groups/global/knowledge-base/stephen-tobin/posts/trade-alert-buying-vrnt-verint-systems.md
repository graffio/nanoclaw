# Trade Alert: Buying VRNT Verint Systems

*Still Waiting for Bitfarms*

### Introduction

VRNT Verint Systems is one of the companies on my Generative AI watchlist. They are an interesting company. Initially, they were the incumbent data center management operation about to be disrupted by new technology and new companies. However, the management has made enormous changes to the company, and they look like they will be a big winner in 2024, all powered by generative AI.

Verint's focus on AI-powered bots and Call center automation is positioned to deliver significant economic benefits, driving towards becoming a Rule of 40 company in three years. 

Verint have many advantages, they are a trusted supplier nad their product can be deployed on the Verint cloud, on any other cloud or at the customers’s premises. I think this offering is unique and will drive significant adoption.

The legacy on-site business has now been transferred to the new SaaS offering, and results are beginning to flow in. It will not be long before Wall Street catches on.

### Last Quarter Highlights

-   Dan Bodner CEO reported Q4 revenue and non-GAAP diluted EPS exceeded expectations, with a 12% year-over-year revenue growth and a 42% increase in non-GAAP diluted EPS.
    
-   Strong SaaS revenue growth at 28% for Q4.
    
-   Bundled SaaS new ACV bookings increased by 16% year-over-year in Q4, and the 12-month pipeline for bundled SaaS grew over 20% year-over-year.
    
-   Verint expects over 40% growth in free cash flow for the current year, with free cash flow growth expected to outpace revenue growth over the next three years.
    

### Strategic Focus and Market Positioning

-   Verint is focusing on CX automation, driven by AI, to address the growing number of interactions and higher customer expectations. 
    
-   The company estimates a significant global workforce involved in customer engagement, presenting a large addressable market for CX automation platforms.
    
-   Verint's differentiation is attributed to its large customer base, data on the platform, and the open platform designed for AI business outcomes. 
    

### Product and Technology 

-   The Verint open platform, including Verint's DaVinci and a team of 40 AI-powered bots, is highlighted for its differentiation and ability to deliver AI business outcomes.
    
-   TimeFlex Bots, an example of Verint's proprietary AI solutions, are designed to improve workforce scheduling flexibility and efficiency. 
    

### Sales Wins and Customer Growth

-   Significant Q4 wins were driven by Verint's differentiated platform, with approximately 50 orders over $1 million TCV each, including major global brands. 
    
-   A notable $49 million TCV order from a leading healthcare company exemplifies Verint's hybrid cloud approach and open platform benefits.
