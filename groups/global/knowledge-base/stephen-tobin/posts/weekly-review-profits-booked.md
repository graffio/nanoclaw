## **Weekly News Digest** _Period covered: 12 – 18 July 2025_

## **D-Wave Quantum Inc. (QBTS)**

-   **Jim Cramer endorsement drives 14 % one-day jump** – CNBC’s Mad Money highlighted D-Wave as a “compelling buy,” extending July’s rally to **+31 %** . Management reiterated that its **gross margin reached 92.5 %** and Q2 net-loss narrowed to the lowest level since listing. The $400 m June ATM raise lifted cash to **$815 m** , which the company calls the strongest balance sheet in quantum computing.
    

-   Zacks Investment Research wrote that earnings-per-share estimates for FY-25 improved from **\-$0.25 to -$0.19** over the last 90 days and maintained a **Hold (Rank #3)** rating.
    

## **Joby Aviation Inc. (ticker: JOBY)**

-   **Dubai commercial launch targeted for 2026** – In an interview with _The Information_ , CEO JoeBen Bevirt confirmed plans to open four “vertiports” in Dubai next year, with initial fares comparable to Uber Black before moving to UberX pricing over time. Production at the California site is doubling to meet demand.
    
-   **Historic piloted eVTOL flights completed in Dubai** – Joby’s electric air taxi finished a series of manned take-offs and landings, marking the start of its market-readiness programme in the UAE. The flights leverage Joby’s 40,000 miles of test history and an exclusive six-year operating agreement with Dubai’s transport authority.
    
-   **Manufacturing capacity expands in California** – Joby now has line-rate capability to build up to **24 aircraft per year** and is ramping a second facility in Dayton, Ohio. The production milestone coincided with the Dubai flight achievements.
    

## **WeRide Inc. (WRD)**

### **Company-issued / Newswire Items**

-   **Southeast Asia’s first fully driverless Robobus service launched** – WeRide began commercial operations on a 12-minute loop at Resorts World Sentosa, Singapore. The service transitioned from safety-officer trials to remote-monitored autonomy after meeting Land Transport Authority assessment criteria. Sensors deliver **360°/200 m obstacle detection** and the programme maintains a zero-incident record.
    
-   The company has also established a dedicated R&D centre in Singapore backed by the Economic Development Board to advance autonomous-vehicle deployment across the region.
    

## **ASP Isotopes (ASPI)**

### **Company-issued / Newswire Items**

-   **First Commercial Shipment** – ASPI will ship its first batch of Carbon14 this month, following previously communicated delays to the timeline.
    
-   **Ytterbium-176 has been successfully enriched** to 99.75% and commercial shipments will begin shortly.
    
-   **The South African Government has issued permits required to import** equipment needed to enrich Gadolinium-160 and Zinc-68
    

## **Key Takeaways**

1.  **Momentum intact across portfolio names** – All three watchlist companies posted operational milestones that support their long-term growth cases.
    
2.  **Capital strength at D-Wave** underpins accelerated R&D and potential M&A, addressing a common liquidity concern in early-stage quantum ventures.
    
3.  **Joby moves from prototypes to scaled production and live flights** , closing the credibility gap for urban air mobility timelines.
