# Portfolio Delivers Standout 85% Return, Sets Sights on 2026 Breakthroughs

*Quantum, Autonomy, Nuclear, Psychedelic in clear view*

As we close the books on 2025, I am proud to report that our portfolio delivered a standout **85% return** for the year—a result that significantly outpaced the broader markets and our primary peer benchmarks. For context, while the **S&P 500** and **Nasdaq Composite** posted respectable gains of approximately **17%** and **20%** respectively, our high-conviction strategy also handily beat the **35%** return of Cathie Wood’s **ARK Innovation ETF (ARKK)** and the **29%** return of **Seeking Alpha’s Quant “Strong Buy”** recommendations. While these results validate the rigorous selection process we maintained through a year of tariff-driven volatility and AI euphoria, I am already shifting my focus to the opportunities of **2026**. The next twelve months will likely be defined by a transition from “experimental” AI to **Agentic AI**—autonomous systems capable of end-to-end execution—alongside breakthroughs in **battery commercialization**, **quantum computing progress**, **recycling adoption and new energy solutions.**

## The Strategy

2026 will be the fourth year of the project (we started on August 1st, 2023). Every year has been a success. We have outperformed both the indices and our chosen peer-group benchmarks by a considerable margin in each trading period. We are clearly in rinse-and-repeat territory. The strategy has proven to be effective and sustainable.

The strategy will not change: identify high-potential emerging technologies, analyse all competitors in the sector, and invest in small-cap players with high potential.

A crucial element is when to invest. These companies are subject to hype and unrealistic expectations, we cannot buy them just because the tech is good, **we buy them because we can make money out of the share price.** If the price is already high or is unlikely to rise for a couple of years, there is no point in buying.

We need the right tech from the right company at the right price.

## Complete results

Although slightly below the 2024 results, when we delivered +100%, it was still an excellent year, with significant profits. The portfolio registered its all-time high in mid-October and, despite a solid December, has not yet recovered to that level. The total return since inception is now 548%

The existing portfolio has substantial exposure to some sectors I think will perform well in 2026, but is underweight in several others. I will be trying to put that right in January and expect a busy Q1 of trading.

The big winners in 2025 were Quantum, Drones and Batteries. In the coming weeks, I will be reviewing each sector and deciding if we should continue investing in these sectors and choosing which company will offer the best potential.
