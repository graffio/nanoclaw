## Company Overview

Electro Optic Systems Holdings is an Australian defence technology company focused on directed energy weapons, remote weapon systems, and counter-drone solutions. Listed on the ASX (ASX:EOS) and also on the Frankfurt exchange. An OTC listing exists in the US as EOPSF, but liquidity is poor and not available on IBKR when Stephen tried to purchase.

**Products:**

- Remote Weapon Systems (RWS) — cannon-based counter-drone systems integrated into multi-layered defence architectures by tier 1 defence primes. US\$22M General Dynamics RWS contract.
- HELW (High Energy Laser Weapon) — the flagship directed energy weapon. EOS remains "the first company to actually sell one, which is quite a big deal." A single HELW unit sells for over \$100M. World-first EUR 71.4M HELW export to a NATO customer.
- Counter-drone interceptor drones — acquired via purchase of a UK company that builds interceptor drones
- "Slinger" counter-drone system — EUR 11.4M NATO order

**Financials (FY2025):**

- Revenue: AUD \$126-128.5M
- Gross margin: 63%
- Cash: AUD \$106.9M
- Backlog: AUD \$459M, expects to convert 40-50% in FY26

**Key contracts/deals:**

- \$3M US order for counter-drone integration by a tier 1 defence prime (Mar 2026)
- \$42M Middle East order for counter-drone integration by a tier 1 defence prime (Mar 2026)
- HELW negotiations underway in the Middle East
- Korean deal worth AUD \$18M pre-payment — finalization pushed to Mar 2026 due to "problems faced by the customer"

**Technology position:** Stephen views EOS as a Directed Energy play. They are the only company to have sold a HELW system, giving them a unique first-mover position. Counter-drone is multi-layered: DroneShield leads in "defeat" (electronic warfare), EOS leads with Remote Weapon Systems (cannons), and the HELW system from EOS remains unique.

**Recent acquisition:** Purchased a UK company that builds interceptor drones, similar to Ukrainian drone interceptors but with the ability to integrate into large-scale defence systems — a capability Ukraine lacks.
