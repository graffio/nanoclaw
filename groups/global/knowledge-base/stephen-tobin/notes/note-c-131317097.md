# Note -- July 2, 2025

Having to change my portfolio name again! It was called the Strong Buy Portfolio, but I got a polite letter from a law firm pointing out that the name was copyrighted. Since then, I have simply referred to it as the Demonstration Account. I thought it was a good name because I am demonstrating my performance; unfortunately, people are interpreting that as it not being a real account.

The account is held, as people who follow my work will know, on Interactive Brokers US. All figures I quote are from that account; it is only a small account (I only invest $250 a month in it) and not my main trading account. Its purpose was to demonstrate the returns of my trading.

From now on, it will be referred to as “The US Trading Account.” Because it is in the US and trades without leverage.

I hope it is not already copyrighted, and people understand exactly what it is from the name. It also fits in with a new account I opened for leverage trading, which I will call “The UK Leverage Account” because it is in the UK trading with leverage.
