# Note -- June 24, 2025

OKLO dropped after a Wall Street analyst finally realized their timeline is nonsense, although Craig-Hallum used the word skeptical rather than nonsense! They are not the only popular stocks with a nonsensical timeline; you can add IONQ, ACHR, and NNE to a growing list. These stocks are likely to burn the retail crowd eventually, but will probably continue rising for the time being, as it is easier to spin a tall tale than to bring a new technology to market.
