## Company Overview

SoundHound AI develops voice AI and conversational intelligence technology for businesses. Their platform enables companies to build voice-enabled products and services, with applications across automotive, restaurants, hospitality, and retail.

**Revenue model:** Enterprise voice AI platform licensing and services.

- FY2025: \$169M revenue (~100% YoY growth)
- Q4 2025: \$55.1M (+59% YoY)
- Non-GAAP gross margin: 60.5%
- FY2026 guidance: \$225-260M
- New Bengaluru innovation hub opened
- "Sales Assist" product launched at MWC

**Key developments:** Stephen's concern centres on customer concentration in consumer-facing verticals — restaurants, retail, and leisure — which are sensitive to macroeconomic downturns.

## Investment Thesis

**Original thesis:** Voice AI platform with strong revenue growth (~100% YoY) and expanding customer base across multiple verticals. Target \$25/share.

**Current view (Feb-Mar 2026):** Stephen classifies SOUN among stocks "falling on no news" — the decline is market-driven rather than fundamental. However, the oil crisis / Middle East escalation introduced new risk: "SoundHound is exposed, as some of its biggest customers are in leisure and retail, both verticals that are likely to be hit hard."

**Concerns:** Customer concentration in discretionary spending sectors (leisure, retail, restaurants). Oil crisis assessment: Short-term risk Medium, Medium-term risk High.
