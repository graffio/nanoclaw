# Spending Spree Ahead.

The prospect list is currently very strong, the market pullback has put numerous companies in the buying zone, and the recent market turmoil has enhanced the market position of some of the technologies we are following. Key technologies are Robotaxis, Quantum, and renewable energy in Europe. I will write in depth about Quantum and Renewable European energy in the coming weeks, but here is an outline of my view on Robotaxis.

The Road Ahead: Navigating the Robotaxi Revolution in 2025

We're always looking at the next big thing, and the robotaxi industry is undoubtedly one of the most compelling. Our "Robotaxi Research 2025 published on SeekingAlpha" delves into this rapidly evolving sector, offering a glimpse into the future of transportation.

**A Market on the Move:** The growth forecasts for the robotaxi market are nothing short of extraordinary. Projections suggest we're on the cusp of a trillion-dollar industry, with some analysts, like Ark Invest, even predicting a staggering $10 trillion market and a $34 trillion increase in equity value. This growth is fueled by the potential for significantly reduced costs per mile, making robotaxis a compelling alternative to traditional transportation.

**Key Players Shaping the Future:** Several companies are at the forefront of this revolution. WeRide, a pioneer in the field, is making strides with its diverse range of autonomous vehicles and international expansion. Pony.AI is focusing on both robotaxis and autonomous trucking, particularly in China, while Waymo, backed by Alphabet, is a recognized leader in the US market. Baidu Apollo Go is a major player in China, and we're also keeping an eye on developments from companies like Zoox (Amazon) and Tesla plus private players like AutoX and auto OEMS like Hyundai.

**Technology and Innovation:** The technology behind robotaxis is complex, involving a combination of sensors like Lidar, radar, and cameras, as well as sophisticated software and AI algorithms. Companies are constantly refining their systems, using both real-world and simulated data to train their autonomous driving capabilities.

**Challenges and Opportunities:** While the potential of the robotaxi market is immense, it's not without its challenges. Companies must overcome regulatory approval, public acceptance, and the ability to scale operations. However, the opportunities are vast for those who can navigate these challenges.

**Looking Ahead:** The robotaxi industry is one to watch closely. As technology advances and business models evolve, we expect to see significant changes in how we move people and goods.

We want to play this in two dimensions, buying the technology providers as well as the robotaxi companies. As always, we want to be early but don't want to suffer a huge drawdown as the industry develops.

I am interested in Hesai and Arbe as technology providers and Pony, WeRide, Baidu, and UBER as robotaxi companies. Waymu and Zoox may also be winners, but their owner companies are so massive that it will likely be some time before the robotaxi division has an impact. It should be noted that they would be a much safer bet than the companies I am considering, but that is the nature of small-cap investing. Tesla faces both risks and opportunities from RoboTaxis I am on the fence regarding their prospects.

I do expect to open small positions in WeRide, Pony and Baidu in the coming weeks but will be looking for some kind of catalyst before I do. Pony and WeRide have seen a near collapse in their share prices, which we like, but I need to see some kind of bottom in place.
