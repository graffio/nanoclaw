# Trade Alert: probably bought a con

DroneShield has continued its collapse overnight in Australia. The company lost the support of its main broker Bell Potter who had been arranging its fund raising but now say they cannot advise people to invest in the company. Australian media is saying a similar thing and the US CEO resigned.

It is a calamity caused entirely by the actions of the CEO and other members of the leadership team deciding to sell 100% of their bonus related shares in one day.

Without market support few companies can prosper in the medium term.

Of course a big order could change things but as they sell in the defence market the loss of market confidence may prompt defence agencies to look elsewhere and if the CEO has lost market trust he may lose customer trust.

A counter argument would say the loss has been taken so why not hold and see what happens? Some may choose that route. If the product is good and the orders arrive the price should recover. I have decided not to hold and will take the hit.

It is a complete disaster and the largest loss I have suffered in many years. I generally am good at spotting these things but they do get through occasionally.

If the stock opens higher in Australia I will put a stop loss at today’s close and see if I can get back a few dollars otherwise I will close at market. I do not have the US ticker.
