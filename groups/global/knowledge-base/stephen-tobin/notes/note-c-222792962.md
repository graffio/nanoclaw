# Note -- March 4, 2026

The word unprecedented is often overused but yesterday was actually unprecedented. Never before has oil gained 7% Gold dropped, bond yields gone up and the S&P fallen 2% as it did before a big recovery in late trading. Oil is up because of supply problems in the Middle East, stocks down on concerns of a drop in global growth because of the Middle East so why did gold drop? Even more why did stocks rally late in the day?
