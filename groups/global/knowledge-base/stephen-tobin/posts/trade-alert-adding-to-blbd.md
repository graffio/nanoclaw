# Trade Alert: Adding to BLBD

*Review Complete and taking opportunity following Pullbacks*

# The Portfolio

The strong buy portfolio was not designed for a Trump presidency. It has two Chinese stocks which may suffer and is very long on EV related companies.

Inevitably, a rotation into other areas will be required in the coming months; however, I do intend to take advantage of market swings that offer good entry points into solid companies.

BlueBird is an example of this. It is a profitable US-based vehicle manufacturer that has seen a notable price fall because 9% of its business is in electric buses; the other 91% seems to be ignored.

BlueBird has a monopoly position in Propane and gasoline-powered Buses, and these might be the biggest beneficiaries of a rollback in EV mandates.

# BYRN

A short seller report has hit Byrn, I am working through the report line by line and checking each fact. So far, I have found nothing of concern and absolutely nothing that contradicts what management has said. I will write up a full report when I finish the work; however, it is time-consuming as I have to telephone companies on Byrns dealer list to check facts.

The only negative is that my relationship with Byrn appears to have gone backwards. They do not appreciate what the term “independent analyst” means. They seemed to think I would do as they asked and not refer to the short report like Wall Street banks do. The idea I would follow a company line is a Joke, I used a four-letter word beginning with F and ending with Off, I don’t think I am on the Christmas list any longer.
