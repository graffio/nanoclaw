## Trade Alert: Buying Airship AI Holdings (AISP)

I first covered AISP on the Seeking Alpha platform in March of this year, when the shares were trading at $3.36, and I rated them a buy. My fair value for the shares was $5.96, not high enough for me to initiate a trade (I look for 100% upside). Since that article, the shares have increased by 51%, I am now rating them a strong buy with a fair value of $17.

The business is growing faster than I had anticipated, approaching cash generation 2 years earlier than forecast. It is benefiting from increased border security spending from its largest customer, the US government. Its commercial customers are increasing their spend, and international governments are signing long-term repeat business contracts.

### AISP The History

Airship AI, originally JDL Digital Systems (2003), went public in December 2023 via a SPAC merger.

Airship AI specializes in "edge" data structuring, utilizing machine learning on sensor data at the source to create searchable databases, thereby eliminating the need for central data centers and AI servers.

### **AISP Operations**

AISP focuses on physical security, using AI to transform so-called “dark data” into searchable information. Dark data is the raw output from cameras, radar, ultrasound, or any other sensor.

**AISP is sensor agnostic;** it will work with any input from any sensor. The sensors can be existing security cams, traffic cams, ring doorbells, robot-mounted LiDARs, drone-mounted radar, or any other sensor you care to mention.

AISP provides three products: one hardware product and two software products.

The system enables users to ask specific questions about video data, and guards do not have to watch a video; they can type in a question. Rather than spending a day watching saved videos, they can type “show me all the red cars passing this point”, or “track this number plate across all cameras in the city”.

Rather than watch a live feed, they could type in “alert me if a human approaches this fence”. Or even “call the police if someone tries to climb this wall.”

### **Outpost AI: The key hardware product**

**Outpost AI** serves as the foundation of AISP's edge computing strategy, functioning as a **purpose-built hardware and software offering** that structures and analyzes data efficiently at the source using Airship AI-trained models. The platform processes unstructured video data into structured outputs, including vehicle images, license plates, make, model, color, locations, and confidence levels, then securely encodes and streams this data to Acropolis for further processing if required.

It doesn't matter if there is low bandwidth or poor connection; the data is structured locally and can be used immediately. At the border wall, for instance, or in the compound office or casino security booth.

This makes scaling the solution very cost-effective, as it works with existing cameras and does not require a data center to store or process the video. At the edge is the lowest cost way to approach this problem

**Acropolis Enterprise Video and Data Management** platform operates as the **backbone of the software ecosystem** , enabling customers to manage anywhere from a handful to hundreds of thousands of devices through a single graphical user interface. The platform supports both on-premises and cloud/multi-cloud architectures, handling structured and unstructured data while performing advanced labeling functions against customer repositories. AISP offers two specialized variations: **Acropolis Commercial** for business applications and **Acropolis Law** for government and law enforcement agencies.

**Airship Command** offers comprehensive **visualization and control capabilities,** spanning workstations, web-based browsers, and mobile applications, ensuring that customer data remains accessible across all deployment scenarios. The platform delivers a "single pane of glass" solution for security operations centers and field personnel. Command is designed to be a city wide monitoring and information gathering platform.

### **Supporting Services**

AISP offers services such as custom AI model training, on-site training, engineering, and remote monitoring.

**Airship AI Holdings (NASDAQ: AISP)** presents a compelling investment opportunity.

### **Strong Financial Performance and Growth Trajectory**

AISP has demonstrated **exceptional revenue momentum** with fiscal year 2024 achieving $23.1 million in net revenue, representing an impressive 87% increase from FY 2023's $12.3 million. The company maintains a **robust financial position** with $8.8 million in cash and cash equivalents as of March 31, 2025, and no debt on the balance sheet. Management targets 30% revenue growth, and I forecast positive cash flow for calendar year 2025, supported by a validated pipeline of approximately $135 million consisting of single and multi-year opportunities across all customer verticals with expected awards throughout the next 18-24 months. Additionally, the company reported a **$2.0 million backlog** on March 31.

Substantial government spending tailwinds, including **$300 million for border security technology** and **$51 million for autonomous towers** in the 2025 appropriations, will likely add to orders in the near term. The **regulatory environment** around border security and public safety creates favorable macro tailwinds, while the company's established relationships with major government agencies provide competitive advantages and barriers to entry for new competitors.

AISP's **commercial market expansion** strategy, particularly targeting organized retail crime, could significantly diversify its customer base. The company's **AI Factory investments** and planned launch of new computer vision analytics and generative AI applications represent important product development catalysts . **International growth opportunities** and potential **strategic M&A activities** provide additional upside potential.

Commercial potential is growing. In January 2025 and October 2024, AISP reported seven-figure contract awards from a [Fortune 100 Transportation and E-Commerce company](https://ir.airship.ai/news-events/press-releases/detail/39/airship-ai-secures-follow-on-seven-figure-contract-award-with-fortune-100-transportation-e-commerce-company).

### **Competitive Position and Market Opportunity**

Operating within a $7 billion edge AI hardware and software addressable market , AISP differentiates itself from traditional surveillance competitors like **Avigilon, Milestone Systems, and Verkada** through its specialized edge AI data management platform that structures unstructured "dark" data in real-time . The company leverages its 19-year operational history and deep domain expertise in AI-driven data management to serve mission-critical applications.

**Key competitive advantages** include established relationships with large early adopters across federal, military, and intelligence agencies, creating significant barriers to entry including security clearances, compliance requirements, and track record necessary for government contracts. The market is demonstrating strong growth, with AI in the video surveillance market valued at $3.90 billion in 2024 and projected to reach $12.46 billion by 2030, at a 21.3% CAGR.

## Forecasting

Having adjusted my model to take account of Q1 results and management guidance, the model has brought forward the breakeven date. I now forecast positive adjusted EBITDA this year and positive net operating income next.

This is a significant change bringing the dates forward almost 2 years and suggests now is the right time to buy, just before profits and as the need for funding comes to an end. I still have a growing share count, as you can see in the model, but the dilution is not enough to affect the path of EPS into positive territory.

It is also worth noting that I have interest turning positive in 2025 as debts are paid off and the cash generated in the late 2024 $8 million equity raise combined with positive earnings eliminates the need for any short term debt.

My forecast for 2026 revenue has increased by almost $10 million due to the expanded order book and the accompanying strong visibility. I have run various scenarios for growth beyond that point and taken an average for my fair value calculation. As we receive more information, I will be able to refine the 2027/8 forecast and publish precise figures when available.

### Risks

We have the usual ones, AISP is still a loss-making small-cap operation. It operates in a large market that will inevitably attract some of the major IT companies. It does have a first mover advantage and a proven track record.

The company suffers from customer concentration and is heavily reliant on orders from the US government. The sensitive nature of the deployments means it will not be able to reveal new orders or discuss the use cases, which makes it difficult to work out exactly what is happening. Execution risks are large both with the government and with its commercial customers, and we do not understand the size of those risks as we are unaware of the terms of any contracts.

They will face supply chain pressure, as much of their operation is based in Taiwan, we have geopolitical concerns. The company has chosen to rely on trade secrets rather than patents, which may be a weakness in its valuation.

## Bottom Line

It is another high-risk, high-potential prospect. It meets all of my investment criteria.

-   Great technology in a market identified as having huge potential.
    
-   First mover advantage, and commercial moat
    
-   Good financial position
    
-   Break even in the forecast horizon
    
-   Proven repeat business from major customers
    
-   New product development opening new verticals.
    

I am going to open a position with a mid-price order to buy 80 shares, which should be a position worth 3% of my equity and meet my $400 position size.
