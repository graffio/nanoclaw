### The Strategic Wave Trading Account

These results are from a trading account I have with Interactive Brokers US. As a UK citizen, it is not my main account but will be used as proof-of-concept and audited before I launch a paid service, hopefully with Seeking Alpha. I initially intended to use my UK account as the proof of concept; however, the accountant I hired to do the audit advised against it as many of my decisions related to my finances rather than the trading situation. For example, in 2022, I sold a large number of shares to buy an RV, and in most quarters, I withdraw money from the account for living purposes.

The Interactive Brokers US account will trade following a set trading plan, and I will refer to it as the Strategic Wave Trading Account. I will neither add to the account nor withdraw from it; in dollar terms, it is very small, and I hope to use the increase in size as a future marketing tool.

I will explain the trading plan in future issues of this newsletter. With the January results I will explain the waves that I use to help with trading.
