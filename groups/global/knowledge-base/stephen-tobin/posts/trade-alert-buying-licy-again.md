# **Li-Cycle: Recent Changes and Investment Outlook**

**Problems**

**Li-Cycle Holdings Corp.**, a global lithium-ion battery resource recovery company, has been in the spotlight due to several significant changes, I wrote about LICY on seeking alpha and gave it a very positive review, since then the share price has collapsed, the causes of the problem were two fold.

1.  **Rochester Hub Project Pause**:
    
    -   In October 2023, Li-Cycle announced a pause in construction work on the **Rochester Hub project**.
        
    -   Escalating costs led to the anticipated aggregate cost of the project exceeding the previously disclosed budget of **$560 million**.
        
2.  **Comprehensive Review and Cash Conservation**:
    
    -   Li-Cycle is actively reviewing options for completing the Rochester Hub.
        
    -   The company aims to conserve cash while evaluating the project’s capital cost, timing, and construction strategy. Work was halted despite much of the facility being almost complete.
        
    -   Simultaneously, Li-Cycle continues to work closely with the **U.S. Department of Energy (DOE)** to secure a **$375 million loan commitment**.
        

**Glencore to the Rescue**

> A sudden surge in share price accompanies news from LICY regarding its finances. In October all appeared lost and Glencore did publish a note saying that it was reviewing its investment

-   **Glencore**, a leading producer, recycler, and marketer of nickel and cobalt for lithium-ion batteries, increased its investment in Li-Cycle yesterday.
    
-   The investment amounts to **$75 million** through a senior secured convertible note.
    
-   This move strengthens Glencore’s endorsement of Li-Cycle’s Spoke & Hub model, patented recycling technology, and development plans for the Rochester Hub.
    
-   The extended maturity and market-based re-pricing of the convertible note provide flexibility for Li-Cycle’s future plans.
    

**Investment Outlook**

I bought yesterday when the news broke and will add to the position today, if LICY can get out of its financial fix then it represents incredible value.

-   **Strong Fundamentals**:
    
    -   Global electric vehicle (EV) production volumes and battery material demand remain robust.
        
    -   Favorable supply and demand dynamics drive the need for domestic sources of battery materials.
        
-   **Circular Economy Focus**:
    
    -   Li-Cycle’s sustainable recycling technology aligns with the growing demand for critical battery materials.
        
    -   The company aims to position itself as a leader in producing these materials.
        
-   **Government Debt Negotiations**:
    
    -   The additional investment by Glencore enhances Li-Cycle’s liquidity position.
        
    -   It demonstrates confidence in the company’s long-term prospects.
        
    -   Li-Cycle’s circularity platform contributes to addressing environmental concerns and government debt negotiations.
        

In summary, I do not want to miss Li-Cycle’s recent changes, Glencore’s investment and the strong market fundamentals that make it a high-risk investment opportunity.
