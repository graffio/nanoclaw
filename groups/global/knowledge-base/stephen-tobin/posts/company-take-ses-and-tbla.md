# Company Take: SES and TBLA

*Holding both despite concerns.*

Earnings season is bringing some hits and misses, we must digest information and make good decisions. SES.AI is down quite a lot since we bought it last month and Taboola has been relatively flat since we invested in the middle of 2024. Neither company has lived up to promise or expectations, but we often have to wait for rewards.

I am always mindful that these small emerging companies can see a share price collapse before making any real progress, and we would rather exit for a small loss than hang on for a big one. It is always a tricky balancing act, the key is not to rush but not to be scared of taking a loss.

# SES.AI

SES delivered an in-line earnings report. Revenue for 2024 was as expected $2 million, and the forward guidance of $20 million (at midpoint) met our forecast.

The earnings call was not a make-or-break event; in fact, it was a bit of a non-event as nothing new or unexpected was disclosed.

We will have to wait and see how the market reacts. However, I would be surprised if this earnings call can stop the general drift lower. The market is not buying this story at the moment, and we are underwater. I will put a soft stop at $0.75 ( by soft stop, I mean I intend to make a close/hold decision at that point, which could be today, the way the price is moving). I will not hang on indefinitely if we close below $0.75 I will take the hit.

2024 was the company's first revenue year, and they have guided to a tenfold increase in 2025. That does represent an excellent start to their commercial journey. Margins are coming in over 60%, a reflection of the product mix, as they have some AI products with almost 100% margins. The CEO said that the forward guidance was based on MOUs and orders already placed, a mixed bag as it gives the possibility of a future miss if the MOUs don't materialize and a beat if they receive additional orders during the year. If the new cylindrical battery lives up to the billing, extra orders could be substantial, and as it is contract-manufactured, SES would find it easy to scale up.

The nature of the companies they have signed as customers for the drone and storage products are not ideal. On the positive side, they have Softbank, which could make SES a ten-bagger on its own. The other companies are little-known with limited trading history so they may not deliver on the MOU's.

The two Auto OEM customers are top ten car manufacturers, and SES still expects them to move to C-Samples this year with the Li-Ion battery. They have signed $10m worth of business for the new AI electrolyte discovery software and to test the newly discovered molecule. These two customers could also turn SES into a ten-bagger if they put SES technology in a production vehicle.

The company has a lot of cash and is guided to enough liquidity to get to 2028; however, they did raise the prospect of a capital raise during the call. Small companies come to market to raise cash to fund their business, so we understand we will suffer dilution when we are early investors.

SES Conclusion

SES still has all the positives that led me to invest in the first place, if I can hold without taking a huge hit I will, but the market will decide if that is possible not me. If we do take a loss SES will remain on my buy list for future investment.

## Taboola (TBLA)

The market reacted badly to this earnings call, with a 20% drop. TBLA is definitely out of favor, and we have had it quite a while without seeing any return. Revenue was below my forecast from last July, I did say that the forecast was difficult to make as TBLA was going through a transformation.

The table below is taken from the press release and shows the transformation from 2023 to 2024. Revenue is up 23%, and net loss is reduced by 95%. In Q4, TBLA delivered a positive income of $33 million, which is well above my forecast.

Conclusion

As with SES, I will continue to hold TBLA-it still has all the positives that made me invest; however, it is not delivering as an investment, so I will place a soft stop at $2.25, but I don't expect that to be hit in the short term.

There are lots more earnings due in the coming days, as well as the Intuitive Machines launch. I will update as news arrives.
