# Trade Alert: Closing Trade 31

*Peace talks likley to reduce market sentiment*

Trade 31 was my investment in British Aerospace, taken on February 25th this year.

At present, it is showing a gain of 45% so it is a successful investment

The rationale for taking it was increased orders, as European nations supplied Ukraine, they would need to restock, and British Aerospace would benefit.

The scenario has now played out, with peace talks taking place, it is likely the main gains for this trade are over.

The company does not fit well into the overall strategy; it is a large, diversified defence company. I will close the trade and look to recycle the cash in the coming weeks.
