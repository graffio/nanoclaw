# **Taboola: Investing in AI-Driven Advertising** 

I continue to move the portfolio to generative AI use cases; this new investment in TBLA is based on several of its product offerings, including its generative AI add-maker that customers can use to develop their ads. The recent earnings call showed that management believes they are on a path to a huge business, and as they continue to sign agreements with high-profile companies, I tend to agree with them.

In today's digital landscape, where data-driven decisions and personalized experiences reign supreme, **Taboola** stands out as a compelling investment opportunity.

1.  **AI-Powered Advertising Revolution**: 
    

-   Taboola's platform is fueled by **artificial intelligence**, making it a game-changer in the advertising industry. Leveraging AI, Taboola optimizes content recommendations, ad placements, and user engagement. 
    
-   **Generative AI Capabilities**: Recently, Taboola unveiled its **Generative AI** for advertisers running campaigns in English. This technology generates ad content, including titles, images, and headlines. Brands benefit from reduced creative production time and high-performing assets. 
    
-   **Efficiency and Effectiveness**: Advertisers can create variations of campaign titles and headlines, experiment with original images, and follow AI-driven best practices. 
    

2.  **The Expanding Advertising Market**: 
    

-   The global advertising market is on an upward trajectory, driven by digital transformation, e-commerce growth, and increased online content consumption. 
    
-   Taboola's platform caters to **600 million daily active users** across websites, devices, and mobile apps. It has long-term partnerships with top digital properties like CNBC, BBC, NBC News, Business Insider, and El Mundo. 
    
-   As advertisers seek innovative ways to reach their target audiences, Taboola's AI-driven recommendations and personalized content play a pivotal role. 
    

3.  **Recent Partnerships and Growth**: 
    

-   Taboola's strategic alliances reinforce its position. Notably, it has partnered with leading media outlets, ensuring brand safety and wide reach. 
    
-   Yahoo was a big signing and in the recent earnings call the CEO said they are about to announce another big name.
    
-   **CNBC**, **BBC**, **NBC News**, **Business Insider**, and **El Mundo** are just a few of the prestigious names in Taboola's network. 
    
-   The availability of Generative AI to all advertisers running English campaigns signifies Taboola's commitment to innovation and client success. 
    
-   Brands like **Babbel** and **Lucid** have already witnessed the impact of Taboola's AI capabilities, achieving better campaign performance and refining their long-term strategies. 
    

The technical chart suggests that now is a good time to invest. There has been a recent pullback, partly driven by a recent shelf registration. This pullback could go quite a bit further, so I have opened the position at half size.
