## Trade Alert: Adding to ACMR

ACMR beat revenue in Q1 and maintained full-year guidance. They have started to extend a factory in Oregon to mitigate tariff pressure for US clients and the proposed on-shoring of silicon chip production to the US should drive sales.

I expect Chinese foundries to continue ramping up silicon production aggressively in response to tariffs. With the majority of ACMR sales based in China, along with its manufacturing facilities, I think demand will remain robust, and we will see continued growth. Interestingly, ACMR is trading at a 60% discount to the value of its 85% stake in ACMR Shanghai. I expect that discount to evaporate in the coming years and it accounts for a lot of my bullish view.

When I took the initial position, I did say the entry point was not ideal; however, this is a very good time that meets all of our criteria. Since taking the trade, we have seen a new high, and a new uptrend appears to be in place.

The original investment was $468, and I will increase that by placing a mid-market order for 3 shares. The price target is unchanged at $55

The bedrock of my original thesis was the EPA funding of new school buses, planned in five rounds. It was designed to deliver $5 billion for the adoption of new vehicles nationwide. The Trump administration halted the dispersal of the money, but reports suggest it is now flowing again. Round 2, which will support revenue this year, resumed delivery in April, and Round 3, which will support revenue in 2026, began being awarded last month. Rounds 4 and 5 remain clouded in uncertainty.

The China tariffs are a headwind for EV sales and likely to increase prices, but BLBD believes customers will change to alternative drive trains (either propane, gasoline, or diesel) rather than cancel or delay. BLBD remains the only manufacturer with a Propane solution. School bus replacement is a safety issue, and a large number of the US fleet are getting old and approaching the end of their lives.

The new commercial chassis appears poised for significant growth, with management indicating strong customer demand. BLBD, with its strong balance sheet and large-scale, experienced manufacturing footprint, is well placed to gain market share.

A modest 5% of this market would account for more unit sales than the school bus section of BLBD. BLBD will be offering a Battery Electric as well as Propane and Gasoline drive trains.
