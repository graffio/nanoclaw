# Trade Alert: Adding to Byrna Technologies

*Due diligence complete*

I have completed my work on Byrna Technologies (BYRN) following last week's knockout earnings call. I am raising my price target to $73 and have just issued a mid price order on IBKR. On the demonstration account, I am buying an additional 6 shares.

We initially bought Byrna for $10.10 last August and saw a 200% increase.

The increased price target comes from adding a new sales channel to my mathematical model and changes to the cost structure.

Byrna delivered a 101% increase in revenue, beating my target of $75 million by $11 million and significantly improving margins. They are now profitable and cash-generating. The buy-back program continues and will support the share price.

If you remember from my first Byrna Post, they make nonlethal handguns that use CO2 to project rubber balls containing irritants that will disable but not kill the target. Their product is unique and far superior to the competition's Chinese imports and toy gun upgrades.

The new store-in-a-store business model with Sportsman Warehouse is an excellent deal and will drive sales going forward. With Byrna only needing to invest $7,500 per store and then have no ongoing costs, it is far better than the original brick-and-mortar plan, which would have cost $110,000 per store per quarter of ongoing expenses.

The store-in-a-store model will begin with 11 sites but quickly expand to more than 100 and has the potential to grow much larger. The US has more than 85,000 sports and hunting stores.

The influencer strategy continues with new big-name political influencers, including Lara Trump, the president's daughter-in-law and successful politician. The influencer strategy led to the 101% increase in revenue in 2024 and will likely continue to drive sales.

Byrna remains banned from social media, but that will likely change very soon; the CEO has appeared on several mainstream TV channels lately, and I expect to see these channels accept Byrna's advertising soon. Surely, the tie-up with the Trump family will cause Facebook to overturn the ban.

Byrna has onshored its entire manufacturing process, including Ammo and only buys plastic ammo tubes from Canada. The 25% tariff would add 7 cents to the cost of production of a complete Byrna kit.

I am currently writing my full article, which will be published on Seeking Alpha in the coming days, but I am buying today.
