# Trade Alert: Buying Centrus Energy

*First Trade of September,*

Centrus appears to have everything going for it, I would rate it Super Strong Buy if I could.

Centrus is a supplier of Nuclear fuel and has:

Strong Balance Sheet, Proven technology, Large Order Book, Proven operational ability, Trained Nuclear Workforce.

I think the next decade will see a huge growth in the Nuclear industry and I want to get in at the beginning. My first thought was to look at the new nuclear companies like NanoNuclear (NNE) and Nuscale Power (SMR), I wrote about SMR. Still, I did not identify an opportunity and I have interviewed one of the Nuclear scientists working as part of the NNE project. Prof Farnan is a Cambridge University professor and currently visiting fellow at Harvard. He provided invaluable information from inside the industry, he explained many of the problems they face and a lot of detail regarding the internal organization at NNE. I cannot imagine buying NNE after speaking with him

I am following 17 companies in my Nuclear Watchlist, the chart below shows some crazy price action in recent days and the wide spread in size of company. It does highlight one crucial point from an investing viewpoint. Very few of these companies currently have meaningful revenue, I am adverse to investing in pre revenue companies, Cameco (CCJ) is to large for my investing plan leaving Centrus Energy (LEU) as the only obvious target. I will continue looking at other companies in the coming months. I am looking at NEE and NEP at the moment who operate Nuclear sites as well as the most extensive clean energy facilities in the world.

I spoke to a contact at Centrus Energy. They firmly believe they will receive the lion share of the $3 billion in awards the government is going to have to develop a US-based enrichment supply chain. Of course firm belief is not fact but he was very confident.

I have interviewed people at NNE and SMR and will not be investing any money in them. I would be better of buying a few lottery tickets, I will never win but the loss will be over quickly.

I have submitted an article to SA and will buy Centrus Energy (LEU) today.
