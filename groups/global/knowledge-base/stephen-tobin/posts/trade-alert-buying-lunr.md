# Trade Alert: Buying LUNR

*NPWR also on the short term buy list*

Very little trading in recent weeks but I opened a position in Intuitive Machines (LUNR) and will likely open a position in NetPower (NPWR) soon.

I have just submitted an article on LUNR to SeekingAlpha, I think they are in an excellent competitive position and recent price action has been responding to news aggressively, up 100% when the IM-1 mission launched, down a chunk when it fell over and up another 13% when they won an additional NASA contract.

IM has three more missions and two more NASA awards pending. 2024 could be a huge year for them. I invested 5% of my equity today.
