# Event: Interview Kobi Marenko CEO ARBE

*A treasure trove of information for small caps investors*

I had the great pleasure of interviewing the CEO of ARBE Robotics and was very impressed by him. He provided a detailed and compelling view of the automotive 4D radar market and answered every question, presenting some real detail. The interview contains a treasure trove of information and is well worth watching. I hope you like it.

# Key Points ARBE

New regulations require all cars in the US to have long-range, all-weather automatic braking, which can only be accomplished with Radar.

Tesla’s camera-only solution is “misleading and cannot be done.” Arbe radar would have solved all the crashes Tesla has suffered so far.

Valeo are no longer working with Arbe, they have signed exclusively with MobilEye.

Uhunder and Continental are an order of magnitude less accurate than Arbe, they are not competitors.

Mercedes has laid a minimum pixel threshold of 1,500. Arbe is way in access of this. Only MobilEye and Arbe can meet this threshold, and other manufacturers are adopting this number.

The ten OEMs in the debt instrument document came from Wikipedia. They are not the same as the list Arbe discussed. Still, they must sign one company from the list to get the money.

Kobi would not discuss BYD, and often, what is not said counts; he was happy to discuss Tesla.

Low-cost EVs will not have LiDar, only cameras and Radar to hit the price point.

The recently announced HiRain deal is in addition to the 300,000-unit pre-order.4

All deals under discussion have volume guaranteed by the Tier 1 and Global Foundries who make the Arbe chipset.

Many thanks to Kobi for sharing half an hour of his time to explain exactly where ARBE is right now and how the market is looking from his point of view.
