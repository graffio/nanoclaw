*Adding EH to my Strong-Buy Portfolio*

I have updated my mathematical model for EHang after the blowout Q2 earnings report and have decided to buy EHang for the third time.

I've submitted an article to SeekingAlpha, which I hope will be published soon. EHang has projected triple-digit revenue growth in the coming years and, due to deposits received, has achieved positive cash flow. The ATM operation has been suspended.

Several new developments have been announced, including a manufacturing partner (GAC), 1,100 new orders, a new manufacturing site, a new HQ, new R&D facilities, and a customer training facility.

The biggest surprise is the reappearance of the VT30, EHang's other aircraft that we have heard little about. The CEO hinted at a surprise announcement regarding this tilt and cruise machine that has similar specs to aircraft from JOBY and ACHR.
